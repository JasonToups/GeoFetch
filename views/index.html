      GeoTask

      GeoTask helps you manage your tasks on a map!

      This is project run by Jason Toups. you can see my repo here

      Show tasks on a Map

      Assign a location to your To-Do list. Go out into the world and accomplish your tasks with ease.

      Manage your tasks

      We provide a clean and easy to use interface for your To-Do lists in both on Map and List views.

      Get notified

      In the future, we will add a notification feature, that will notify you when you're close to any location that has a To-Do list.
